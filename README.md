# 🍪 Cookie Run

A 2D platformer made in Godot 4. You're a cookie lost inside a giant gingerbread house — jump across platforms and find your way out.

## Controls

- `A` / `D` or `← →` — Move
- `Space` or `↑` — Jump

## How to Play

Navigate through the gingerbread house by jumping across platforms. Reach the exit to win.

## Made with

- Godot 4
- GDScript
- Aseprite
